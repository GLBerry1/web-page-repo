About Us

Acontract manufacturing (CM) company that namufactures electronic circuit boards for larte OIMs (Original Equipment Manufacturers). The OEMs design the hardware and circuits, and the CMs build the boards using the designs provided. The CMs use state-of-the-art manufacturing assembly techniques which include automated assempl lines where machines place the parts on the pre-printed circuit boards, send them through ovens where the parts are soldered to the boards, and wash the boards after they are soldered. The process invovles set ups of the placement machines for each board; programming the profiles of the "ovens" so the empwratures are appropriate for the boards. After the boards come off the lines, they are tested using Automated Test equipment. Failures are tracked and brought to an in-house Failure Analysis lab to determine why the parts are failing and to determine whe in the procee probles might be occurring.
